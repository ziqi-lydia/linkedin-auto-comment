An AI skill that automates meaningful LinkedIn engagement across your feed -- so you show up consistently, build real connections, and never miss a relevant opportunity.

**Built for two types of people:**
- **Job seekers** who need to stay visible to hiring managers and recruiters without spending 2 hours a day on LinkedIn
- **B2B founders & content creators** who need to manage engagement at scale while keeping it personal

## What It Does

### For Job Seekers

**1. Smart Feed Scanner**
Sai scrolls your LinkedIn feed every day, finds posts from the last 12 hours that match your target positions, and leaves a genuine one-liner comment + your email. Many hiring posts ask candidates to "drop your email below" -- Sai handles this automatically so you never miss a window.

> *Example: You're targeting Product Manager roles in AI. Sai finds a VP of Product's post saying "We're hiring PMs -- drop your email." Sai comments: "Exciting opportunity! 3 years of PM experience in AI/ML products. Would love to connect -- sarah@email.com" -- all while you sleep.*

**2. Opportunity Matcher + Outreach Drafter**
Sai identifies posts mentioning "open roles," "we're hiring," or "looking for" -- then checks whether the job description aligns with your background and career goals. For strong matches, it drafts a personalized outreach message highlighting how your experience maps to the JD, and includes a referral request.

> *Example: A recruiter posts about a Senior Data Scientist role at Stripe. Sai cross-references your resume, finds 80% skill overlap, and drafts: "Hi [Name], I saw your post about the Sr. Data Scientist role -- my 4 years of ML infrastructure experience at [Company] maps closely to what you're describing. Would you be open to a quick chat or a referral? Here's my resume: [link]"*

---

### For B2B Founders & Content Creators

**3. Comment Triage + Auto-Reply**
Every day, Sai checks all comments on your recent posts. For low-priority comments (generic praise like "Great post!" or "Love this!"), Sai auto-replies in your personal tone. For high-priority comments -- from large accounts, frequent engagers, or substantive questions -- Sai flags them for your personal attention.

You define what "low-priority" and "high-priority" mean:
- Commenter has 10K+ followers? → Flag for personal reply
- Commenter has engaged with 3+ of your posts? → Flag
- Comment is just "Great post" or an emoji? → Auto-reply

> *Example: You wake up to 47 comments on yesterday's viral post. Sai has already replied to 38 generic ones ("Thanks! Glad it resonated" / "Appreciate that 🙏") and flagged 9 for you -- including a reply from a VP at your target account and a question from someone with 50K followers.*

**4. Industry Topic Engagement**
Sai searches LinkedIn for posts in your industry or your company's service area, then leaves thoughtful, insightful comments in your voice -- not generic "Great post!" but real takes that demonstrate expertise. This builds your visibility with potential customers, partners, and thought leaders.

> *Example: You run a fintech startup. Sai finds a post about "why traditional banks are losing to neobanks," reads it, and comments in your voice: "The real gap isn't features -- it's onboarding speed. We've seen 3x conversion when time-to-first-transaction drops below 90 seconds. Banks could close this gap with better API orchestration."*

## Quick Start

1. Tell Sai: **"Set up LinkedIn auto comment"**
2. Sai asks you a few questions:
   - Are you a job seeker or a creator/founder?
   - What's your target role / industry / company focus?
   - What tone should comments use? (Professional, casual, witty?)
   - What counts as "important" vs. "auto-reply" for comment triage?
3. Sai runs daily on your schedule -- or you can trigger it anytime with **"Check my LinkedIn"**

No API keys. No browser extensions. Sai uses your actual LinkedIn session in your browser.

## Features

- **Feed scanning** -- finds relevant posts from the last 12 hours based on your keywords and goals
- **Smart commenting** -- context-aware comments, never generic spam
- **Email drop** -- auto-comments your email on hiring posts that ask for it
- **Opportunity matching** -- cross-references job posts against your resume/background
- **Outreach drafting** -- personalized referral requests for strong-match opportunities
- **Comment triage** -- separates noise from signal in your post comments
- **Auto-reply** -- handles low-priority comments in your voice
- **Priority alerts** -- flags high-value comments for your personal response
- **Industry engagement** -- proactive commenting on relevant industry posts
- **Tone matching** -- learns your writing style from your existing posts/comments
- **Rate limiting** -- built-in safety (max 20-30 comments/day, random intervals) to keep your account safe
- **Daily digest** -- summary of everything Sai did: comments left, opportunities found, replies sent

## Safety & Compliance

LinkedIn has strict automation policies. This skill is built with safety-first design:

- **Human-like pacing**: 60-120 second random intervals between actions
- **Daily caps**: Maximum 20-30 comments per day (configurable)
- **No connection spam**: This skill only comments -- no mass connection requests
- **Your review**: All high-priority interactions are flagged for your approval
- **Session-based**: Uses your real browser session, not headless bots
- **Natural patterns**: Varies comment length, tone, and timing to avoid detection

## How It Works

1. **Setup**: You tell Sai your goals, target keywords, and preferences
2. **Scheduling**: Sai runs at your chosen time (or on-demand)
3. **Scanning**: Opens LinkedIn in your browser, scrolls the feed, reads posts
4. **Matching**: Uses AI to evaluate relevance against your profile
5. **Engaging**: Leaves contextual comments, drafts outreach, triages replies
6. **Reporting**: Delivers a digest of actions taken

## Privacy

- Sai operates in your own browser session -- no credentials are stored or shared
- Your resume and target preferences stay on your local machine
- No data is sent to third parties
- Comment drafts are generated locally before posting
